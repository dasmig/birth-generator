The demographic data in the resources/ directory is derived from:

1. United Nations World Population Prospects 2024 Revision
   (https://population.un.org/wpp/)
   Used under United Nations terms of use.

2. United Nations Demographic Yearbook — Statistics Division
   (https://unstats.un.org/unsd/demographic-social/products/dyb/)
   Used under United Nations terms of use.

3. World Bank Open Data (https://data.worldbank.org/)
   Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0).
   https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

4. World Health Organization Global Health Observatory
   (https://www.who.int/data/gho)
   Used under WHO terms of use.

You are free to share and adapt the data for any purpose, including
commercial use, provided you give appropriate credit to the original sources.
